Department of Safety and Inspections


Who we are

A proactive, multi-disciplinary safety and inspections organization that builds and maintains a livable, Saint Paul community through strong leadership, creative partnerships and effective regulatory strategies.

Our Mission and Vision

To preserve and improve the quality of life in Saint Paul by protecting and promoting public health and safety for all. To be the leader in creating the safest, healthiest, most livable, and vibrant City in America.

Our Values

  • Serve our customers with respect and dignity.
  • Act with integrity and professionalism in all interactions with our customers and the public at large.
  • Commit and dedicate ourselves to public service.
  • Embrace the diversity of our City and our organization, valuing different viewpoints, expertise, and cultures.
